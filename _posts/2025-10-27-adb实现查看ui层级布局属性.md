获取并生成xml
`adb shell uiautomator dump --compressed`

拉取xml到adb目录
`adb pull /sdcard/window_dump.xml`

截屏
`adb shell screencap -p /sdcard/test.png`  

拉取图片到adb目录
`adb pull /sdcard/test.png`
